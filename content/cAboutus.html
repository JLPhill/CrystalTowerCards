Lore

Crystal Tower Cards is an online pop-up store that specialises in multiple collectible Trading Card Games(TCG). Some of these include Final Fantasy Trading Card Game(FFTCG), Pokemon Trading Card Game(PTCG) and Digimon Trading Card Game(DTCG).

We keep up to date on upcoming tournaments within Australia, information on the latest expansions, new strategies and fun deck ideas to explore.

We hold products for both new players and veterans of the trading card games including rare cards, singles , promos and other box sets. Being fans of the Final Fantasy franchise, we also stock other merchandise related to main titles and characters within the series.
